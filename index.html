Year 9 Music

Welcome to Year 9 Music 2020!

Our aim is to develop our skills in playing, composing, and listening through a range of musical contexts over the next 2 Terms

Achievement Objectives

You will be working in level 4 of the four strands:

  • Developing Practical Knowledge in Music (PK)
  • Developing Ideas in Music (DI)
  • Communicating and Interpreting Music (CI)
  • Understanding Music in Context (UC)

Course Structure

Three periods per week for half a year. Each week will usually consist of:

  • One period - Music theory focus
  • One period - Music aural focus
  • One period - Practical work focus

Units covered during this course:

Unit One: Guitar and Keyboard Skills

Unit Two: Composition and Song-Writing

Unit Three: Digital Music
