Guitar and Keyboard Unit

Purpose

The purpose of this unit is to give you a basic understanding of how to play the acoustic guitar and keyboard.

What will I learn?

When learning a new instrument there are a range of things to learn. By the end of this unit you should become familiar with basic shapes and patterns for playing chords fluently. You will then be able to apply this skill in playing multiple songs.
